About Laptop

A laptop is a portable computer that combines the components and functionality of a desktop computer in a compact, lightweight design. It typically includes a screen, keyboard, trackpad or mouse, and built-in battery, allowing for easy mobility and use on the go. Laptops are used for a wide range of activities, including work, entertainment, gaming, and learning, and they come in various sizes, specifications, and performance capabilities. Their versatility and convenience make them essential tools for modern digital life.
